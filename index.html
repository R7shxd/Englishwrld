Course Curriculum

A step-by-step journey from sounds to fluent speech.

Module 1: The Foundation of Clear Speech

Understanding the mechanics of American pronunciation and training your ear to hear the key phonetic and prosodic differences.

Module 2: Mastering Core Consonants

Intensive drills for the American R, TH sounds (θ/ð), W vs. V distinction, and the natural-sounding Flap T/D.

Module 3: Vowel Precision & Reduction

Targeted practice for the Long O (/oʊ/), Dark L (/ɫ/), and mastering the Schwa (ə) through vowel reduction in unstressed syllables.

Module 4: The Music of Speech - Stress & Rhythm

Learning to identify and use content vs. function words, apply default stress patterns, and speak in natural thought groups.

Module 5: The Melody of Speech - Intonation & Linking

Mastering how pitch changes meaning, emotion, and contrast. Learn to connect words smoothly for effortless flow.

Module 6: Real-World Fluency

Applying all your new skills to phrases, sentences, and conversational practice scenarios to build lasting confidence.
